Profile

As an extended wing of Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya Colleges, Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering was established to provide multi disciplinary education facility in the same campus by The Society for Collegiate Education in July 2010 with the prior approval of AICTE, Government of Andhra Pradesh & Andhra University. The college endeavors to meet the strong demand in industries and research organizations for Competent Engineers, Proficient Technologists and Creative Scientists. The Campus is ideally located in the heart of the city on 10 acre land and it boasts of providing various services, facilities and amenities for the all round progress of inquisitive learners.




Broad Aims and Objectives of the College

• The college aims to provide the right opportunities to engineering students for learning the new technologies and building the extraordinary careers in industry and research organizations
• Strives to develop human virtues such as work culture, expertise, communication and social values.
• Endeavors to promote technical education and encourage activities of Social Importance and Environmental Awareness



Salient Features

• Highly disciplined institution imparting Quality Technical Education in the fields of Engineering & Technology.
• Centrally located in the City of Destiny and surrounded by Andhra Pradesh IT HUB
• Top Class Infrastructure, Equipment and Amenities for all academic activities in our campus.
• Innovative and resourceful training by Proficient professors
• Continuous Assessment of Academic progress of students and Teaching faculty by IQAC(Internal Quality Assurance Cell)
• Periodical Seminars and workshops on the latest developments in Engineering Disciplines, Technological revolution and entrepreneurial skills.
