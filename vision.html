Department Vision

To become an educational ecosystem that acts as a beacon for those inclined towards solving societal problems in an inclusive, ethical and technologically relevant manner and steer them towards national development.



Department Mission

To provide the students with the overall knowledge base essential to enable them to choose domains in which they would like to be trained sufficiently in order to achieve their personal and professional goals.


To bring about an awareness in the minds of the students regarding the right approaches to be followed to acquire the necessary skills in the chosen domain.

To inculcate the philosophy of Continuous and Never Ending refinement and so that these skills are refined so as to match industry requirements.

To evolve a system of content creation, delivery and testing so as to match global educational standards.

To create an environment conducive for collaborative research, participatory and student-centric learning, and innovation.
